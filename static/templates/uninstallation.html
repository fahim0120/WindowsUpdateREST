Study to Measure Daily Computer Power Usage



Software Uninstallation Manual




Step 1: Click on the Windows search box and search for “Add or remove programs”.
Select the “Add or remove programs” option.  
 

Step 2: Search for “PowerMeterApp”. Select the “PowerMeterApp” option.  
 

Step 3: Click on the Uninstall button.  
 

Step 4: Click on the OK button. Leave the “Automatically close applications and attempt to restart them after setup is complete” option selected.  
 

Step 5: Click on the Yes button.
